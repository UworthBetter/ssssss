# P3-001 subject center shell

This note records the first subject-center slice that upgrades the original
subject management page into an object-center shell with a 360 preview.

## Goal

Keep the existing subject CRUD flow stable while establishing the first
platform-level subject-center structure.

## What this slice adds

- shared platform shell on the subject page
- subject summary cards
- risk-level tags in the main list
- selected-subject 360 preview panel
- device summary placeholder
- event summary placeholder
- AI tag placeholder block
- lightweight subject timeline

## What remains out of scope

- full subject detail route
- real device linkage data
- real event linkage data
- intervention records
- full AI profile detail

## Why this matters

This slice moves the subject page from flat management toward a true object
center without forcing a risky full rewrite.
